The problem

El conocimiento que opera sus líneas
hoy vive en una sola cabeza.

En una operación de 950,000 toneladas anuales distribuidas en 30 plantas, la consistencia de sus procesos de tratamiento de superficies depende de operadores expertos que acumularon su conocimiento durante años. Ese conocimiento no está documentado. No se transfiere. Y cuando ese operador se va, se va con él.

01
Expert leaves = line that wobbles

When the expert operator leaves (retirement, job change, rotation), the replacement takes 6 to 18 months to reach the same consistency level. During that time, scrap increases, customer complaints grow, and the line runs at reduced capacity.

02
Static FMEA = dead FMEA

An FMEA in Excel or on paper goes stale in weeks. Failure modes evolve with every supplier change, every new part, every process adjustment. If the FMEA doesn't update itself with actual operations, it's not a quality tool — it's a compliance checkbox.

03
Late detection = full lot compromised

The phosphate bath pH drifted at 10:17 am. At 11:42 am someone noticed. Those 85 minutes of parts are scrap or rework. By the time a defect is caught visually at the end of the line, 30 to 200 parts have already been processed under the same bad condition.

The solution

SurfaceOS: your live FMEA, lot-level traceability,
and the operator who never leaves.

SurfaceOS es un sistema de monitoreo industrial que captura, procesa y comunica el estado de sus líneas en tiempo real, convirtiendo el conocimiento tácito de sus mejores operadores en un activo digital permanente.

📡

Real-time visibility

Critical variables at every stage (pH, temperature, voltage, chain speed, humidity, film thickness) monitored continuously. Leadership, quality, operations, and maintenance all see the same information from any device. For the first time, the complete status of your lines is visible without walking the floor.

📋

Live FMEA with lot-level traceability

Every failure event is documented with photo, process variables, affected parts, corrective action, and response time. The system learns from each incident. Every lot ships with an automatic process certificate: full traceability with no additional work from the quality team.

🔔

Smart notifications and measurable MTTR

When a variable goes out of range, the system calculates how many parts were processed under that condition, notifies the shift supervisor via WhatsApp, and records time from detection to corrective action. That MTTR becomes an operational KPI that was previously impossible to measure.

Roadmap

Increasing value over time.

SurfaceOS is not a one-time implementation project: it is a platform that grows with your operation. Each phase adds capabilities without replacing what came before.

1
Mes 1 – 2

Visibilidad

The foundation. The system sees everything happening on your lines from day one.

  • Sensor installation on both lines
  • Live dashboard accessible from any device
  • Alerts configured by threshold and variable
  • Failure capture: photo + variables + response time
  • Lot traceability operational from day one
2
Mes 3 – 6

Inteligencia

The system learns from your operation and starts predicting before failures occur.

  • Correlación automática condición → falla

  • Reportes automáticos para calidad y dirección

Mes 6+
